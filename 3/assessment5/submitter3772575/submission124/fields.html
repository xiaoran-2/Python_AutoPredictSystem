  • one of the biomarkers of human cancers and of cardiovascular disease is REs & repetitive eliments  hypomethylation & genomic instability.
  • hypomethylation of REs/repetitve eliments is more common than hypomethylation of CPG poor promoters which results in Genomic Instability via allowing Illegitimate recombination between repeats, Activation of repeats and transposition and Activation of cryptic promoters and disruption to neighbouring genes.
