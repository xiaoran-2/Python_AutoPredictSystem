Occurs at CpG dinucleotides.Helps to maintain genomic stability.Mitotically heritable, due to features of DNMT1 .Can be removed passively, or actively which involves TET proteins .Essential for viability, as DNMT knockouts die in utero. epigenetic changes are passed on during cell division to daughter and granddaughter cells until they are actively erased. Once erased, though, they do not return. It might therefore be that epigenetic therapies can effect changes which stop a cancer growing without having to kill all its cells.
